XV Top 5 Games

These 5 games are the ones that were the most fun to play and enjoy. As a kid growing up and revisiting/now playing these games.

  1. Legend Of Zelda: Breath of The Wild

    This ranks up to the very top being an industry changing game, using the nintendo switch in a few ways to enhance the player experience. The story is very stable and provides an honest journey to enjoy with or without help. Overal 10/10 having great qualities that envelop the best parts of gaming all in one.

  2. Kingdom Hearts Birth By Sleep

    Previously only available for PSP, now on every platform today ranking high with story and nostalgia using Disney and Final Fantasy-esque characters to build their stories makes this a truly remarkable game.

  3. Bioshock 1

    A true classic from Xbox 360 age, the eerie story, the combat and points system all make up integral parts that make this a masterpiece.

  4. Batman: Arkham City

    This game has excerptional combat, puzzles and story. Definitely up there due to the DLC though.

  5. Persona 5 Royal

    The constant use of the life simulation mixed with the action, adventure and the storytelling Atlus is well known for makes this a great game.
